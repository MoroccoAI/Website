About US

MoroccoAI is a professional network which gathers Moroccan professionals and researchers in Artificial Intelligence to empower Morocco’s presence in Artificial Intelligence

Vision

Our vision is to build the leading professional Moroccan Artificial Intelligence network to empower Morocco's presence in Artificial Intelligence, and promote excellence in Artificial Intelligence Education, research and innovation

Mission

Our mission is to support Artificial Intelligence researchers and practitioners in Morocco, empower Moroccan Artificial Intelligence practitioners with cutting-edge advancements in AI, and to provide opportunities for Moroccan students and practitioners

Values

We strive to uphold the guiding principles that make us who we are and shape where we’re headed



  • Diversity and Inclusion


  • Education and Innovation


  • Transparency
