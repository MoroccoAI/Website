Sponsors Request

The Deep Learning IndabaX-Morocco 2019, is the first Deep Learning event in Morocco supported by the African Deep Learning Indaba. It is a locally-organized Indaba that aims to empower the community of Artificial Intelligence in Morocco.

The event will take place at the Faculty of Sciences and Techniques of Mohammedia (FSTM) in order to share advanced knowledge of Deep Learning to students, researchers and industry professionals. The event will bring together local and Moroccan diaspora experts to expose students to state-of-the-art methods and practical skills in AI advanced technologies.

Your organization is invited to support Deep Learning Indaba X Morocco 2019. This is an ideal way to demonstrate your organization's commitment to the field of artificial intelligence including Deep Learning in Morocco, and many other areas at the frontier of technological innovation and to publicize this support to many leaders and students in the field. Exhibits by sponsors also help inform researchers and engineers about relevant applications and opportunities.

Your support is an investment in the next generation of Moroccan talents in AI. It removes barriers to participation and will typically be used to permit a greater number of students to attend Indaba X Morocco free of charge.

Your support can be targeted to a particular event or activity at the event. You can, for example, propose awards for the data challenge and 3 Minutes Thesis activities, etc. We welcome any innovative ideas that you might have for us. We are open to discussing modifications or additional sponsorship opportunities as well. For further information, please contact us.

The organizing committee ensures that these contributions are well recognized. We hope that you will take advantage of this great opportunity to sponsor and/or exhibit your products, services and brand to a large audience of specialists in Artificial Intelligence in Morocco.
