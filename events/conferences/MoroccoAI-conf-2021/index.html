About The Event

The first edition of MoroccoAI annual conference (MoroccoAI 2021) will be held in December XX-XX, 2021. It will bring advanced knowledge and recent developments in AI for students, researchers and industry professionals. In addition to the scientific program, the conference will also include plenary sessions, Workshops and Data challenges. Attending the conference is a great opportunity for participants for networking and debating about different fields of AI.

Main objectives

  1. Advance the state of R&D in the field of AIin Morocco
  2. Build a dynamic Moroccan AI community with the aim to educate, connect and support AI practitioners
  3. Foster more collaboration between academia and industry
  4. Increase the number of Moroccan representation in top AI conferences
