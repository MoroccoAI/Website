Panel Description

There is tremendous promise in the possibilities that AI offers in transforming industries and countries economic growth and development. Research is at the core of AI advancements; thereafter, companies, governments, universities, NGOs, and others around the world have made substantial investments in Research and Development (R&D), which gave rise to unprecedented innovations. This panel will debate the current state of AI and how it is shaping the world around us. Panelists will share their perspectives on what drives innovation and the role of R&D in the current Fourth Industrial Revolution. The panel will also discuss how to bridge the gap between research in academia and industry.

The panel promises you an erudite discussion between a diverse set of panelists with a rich background around the topic and decades of expertise in the AI space.
