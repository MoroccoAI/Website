Call for Data Challenge

MoroccoAI Annual Conference 2021 is organizing a data challenge to offer an opportunity to anyone interested in machine learning to learn and test ML skills on a real-case problem.

This data challenge addresses the problem of Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) in Moroccan licensed vehicles. Based on a small training dataset of 450 labeled car images, the participants have to provide models able to accurately recognize the plate numbers of Morocco licensed vehicles.

How to Participate?

  1. Fill the form
  2. Receive team single identifier
  3. Receive link to competition

Rules of Participation

  • The participants are encouraged to take further pictures of vehicles in different situations and environments, label them and add them to the training set in order to increase the classification accuracy of their models.
  • Freely & publicly available external data is allowed, including pre-trained models.
  • Don't cheat!
  • Apply yourself!
  • Have fun!

Prizes

The following prizes are offered by the ANRT (Agence Nationale de la Réglementation des Télécommunications) and thanks to the Ministry of Digital Transition of Morocco. In addition, Nvidia is offering credits for training courses and certifications by Nvidia Deep Learning Institute (DLI) The prizes will be awarded on the basis of private leaderboard rank.

  1. 1st Prize : Award of $1400 value and Nvidia DLI Credits
  2. 2nd Prize : Award of 600$ value and Nvidia DLI Credits

During the conference, a special session will be organized where the two winning solutions to the Data Challenge will be presented and the prizes will be offered to the winning teams.
