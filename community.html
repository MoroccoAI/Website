Welcome to MoroccoAI community, an initiative led by AI experts in Morocco and abroad to promote AI growth in Morocco across the spectrum.


We host diverse AI events including webinar series, podcast series, study groups and papers readings to promote excellence in AI education and research. We foster aspirational efforts in AI for Morocco’s socially beneficial purposes through connecting people and buidling a strong communtiy.

Guided by our values and our Moroccan DNA, we embrace a culture of sharing, learning and taking the lead to make impact.

  • We are committed to include individuals from different disciplines and backgrounds, to feel respected, motivated, valued and recognized;
  • We are committed to promoting AI transformation in Morocco while maintaining objectivity and quality performance;
  • We encourage bold ideas and strive for excellence through continuous improvement.


This is your community, so feel at home and join Slack to connect with your fellow Moroccans, exchange ideas, build collaborations, and lead your initiatives.
Most importantly do not forget to subscribe to our MAILING LIST below.
